Our Story

Baked With Tradition
& Heart

What started as a humble family kitchen in the heart of Baramati has grown into a beloved institution with 8 locations across the city. For over 14 years, we've woken before dawn to fill Aishwarya Bakery with the warm scent of freshly baked pastries, breads and cakes.

We believe in slow fermentation, quality flour, and recipes passed down through generations — because great bread simply cannot be rushed.

100% Natural Ingredients
No preservatives — pure, wholesome ingredients sourced locally every morning.
Traditional Stone-Deck Oven
Our heritage oven gives every loaf its signature golden crust and chewy crumb.
Made With Love, Daily
Fresh batches every morning — if it isn't fresh, it doesn't leave our kitchen.
