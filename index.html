Our Story

Baked With Tradition & Heart

What started as a humble family kitchen in the heart of Baramati has grown into a beloved bakery with 8 locations across the city.

For years, Aishwarya Bakery has woken before dawn to fill the city with the warm aroma of freshly baked cakes, pastries, breads and khari.
